Garlic Parmesan Chicken wings

By Kevin Curry a.k.a FitmanCook

Quick recipe that goes well with almost everything; good for football days.

Ingredients

  • 4lb of chicken wings, fat trimmed
  • 2 tbsp of the Fitcook everyday blend
  • Garlic
  • Mustard
  • Cumin
  • Ground herbs
  • 4 tbsp of the Fitcook garlicky herb land blend
  • Pinch of salt & pepper
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1/3 cup reduced fat grated parmesan

    • How to cook

  1. Trim the fat from the wings
  2. Season the chicken with the Fitcook everyday blend and the Fitcook garlicky herb land blend
  3. Put the wings on a baking tray, and spray some olive oil
  4. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes at 420F
  5. Mince some garlic and parsley, then put it on the chiken
  6. Mix well, then add the garlic parmersan
  7. Enjoy!!!
